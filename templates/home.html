WHY IS IT WORTH TAKING THE TEST?

While this quiz is not a diagnosis for autism, the test can give you an indication of whether your child has traits of autism. If you score highly on this quiz, you may wish to speak to your GP about arranging a formal diagnosis. A diagnosis can help you to access the support your child needs and can help explain to others what this support should be.
